# Document Upload

Uploaded documents help the app understand runbooks, business rules, known issues, and approved fixes.

Navigation path: Documents -> Upload document.

Steps:
1. Open Documents.
2. Select workspace.
3. Enter a clear title.
4. Choose a supported file.
5. Click Upload document.
6. Ask AI Chat questions after the upload completes.

Warnings:
- Upload only documents that belong to the selected workspace.
- Avoid unnecessary sensitive personal data in documents.
